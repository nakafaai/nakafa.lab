## Current Findings

Adult mitapivat context is stronger than the early research map: FDA sources
support adult AQVESME context, and Agios announced European Commission approval
for adult thalassemia on 2026-05-22. This still does not prove local
availability, affordability, safety fit, or individual fit.

The May 23 ClinicalTrials.gov API check keeps pediatric mitapivat as
watchlist-only: `NCT07506863` pediatric TDT and `NCT07517133` pediatric NTDT are
not yet recruiting and have no posted results. `NCT04770779` adult TDT remains
active-not-recruiting with posted results and stays adult benchmark context.

## Current Blockers

  bank, iron/organ risk, and referral readiness still need clinician validation.
- Mitapivat context remains blocked from patient-specific interpretation until
