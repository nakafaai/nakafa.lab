# Quran Anchor: Al-Hujurat 49:6

Date checked: 2026-05-10
Evidence label: Quran research ethics, not biomedical evidence

## Scope

This note documents Quran 49:6 as a verification guardrail for public reports,
links, and claims. It does not identify a thalassemia treatment, molecule,
trial, supplement, or clinical action.

## Working Reading

The verse warns believers to verify consequential reports before acting, so that
harm is not caused through ignorance. For Nakafa Lab, the research-method lesson
is narrow: public claims, registry links, and confident replies must be checked
before they influence a patient-facing pathway.

## Fit With Thalassemia Research

This anchor strengthens four rules:

- verify official registry IDs before discussing trial context;
- classify TDT, NTDT, iron-axis, transplant, gene-cell therapy, and follow-up
  records separately;
- route clinical claims to qualified owners;
- keep uncertainty visible until source checks are complete.

## Guardrail

Do not use this verse as biomedical evidence. Use it to slow down unsafe jumps
from public claim to clinical conclusion.
