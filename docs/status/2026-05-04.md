## Current Practical Goal

Contact one owner at a time with one narrow ask:

1. hematology or thalassemia clinic: confirm diagnosis wording, dependence
   category, and missing records;
2. transfusion service or blood bank: define burden and immune/blood-bank record
   needs;
3. molecular genetics or genetic counseling: define molecular confirmation and
   modifier-record needs;
4. iron, MRI, cardiology, endocrine, or organ-monitoring owner: define iron and
   organ-risk packet completeness;
5. transplant, gene-cell therapy, or trial team: define whether screening is
   ready, partial, blocked by missing data, access-blocked, medically unsuitable,
   or under review.

## Current Blockers

- Diagnosis and molecular subtype remain `phenotype_only` until the right owner
  confirms or corrects the label.
- Transfusion burden remains unquantified until private log fields are complete
  enough to calculate volume, red-cell fraction, hemoglobin response, and iron
  input.
- Immune and blood-bank interpretation remains blocked until antibody,
  DAT/direct Coombs, matching, reaction, spleen, and hemolysis context are
  reviewed.
- Iron and organ-risk interpretation remains blocked until ferritin trend, LIC,
  cardiac `T2*`, chelator monitoring, and organ screening are packeted.
- Advanced therapy and trial relevance remains blocked until a qualified owner
  reviews the packet.

## Practical Rule

Do not send the full paper as the first artifact. Send the owner-specific row,
then attach private records only outside the public repository.
