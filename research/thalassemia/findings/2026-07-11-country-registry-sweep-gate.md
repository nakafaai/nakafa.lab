finding: country-specific queries are required for Asia-facing thalassemia
surveillance.

The sweep queried active-status `beta thalassemia` records across 15 country
terms. It recovered 30 unique registry IDs across 10 country queries.

## Practical Meaning

The public outreach ask becomes:

> If you know thalassemia programs in one of these countries, please share a
> public registry ID, guideline, official program page, or PMID, plus the
> country/status and the qualified owner who should interpret it.

The repo should still reject public replies that jump directly to treatment
selection, trial enrollment, travel, import, purchase, private-record exchange,
lab contact, or sample routing.

## Islamic Motivation Boundary

Quran 49:6 supports verification before acting on consequential reports. Quran
4:58 supports routing entrusted questions to their proper owner. Quran 16:43
supports asking qualified people when knowledge is missing. These are process
ethics only and are not biomedical evidence for registry status, eligibility,
referral, treatment, import, purchase, or clinical action.
