# Daily Research Journal: 2026-05-03

Audience: founder / engineer
Status: active research summary, not a final conclusion and not medical advice

## TL;DR

Nakafa Lab has not found a new medicine.

Today the highest-value blocker is specialist-owner routing. After a doctor
reply, each unresolved domain needs the right clinical owner instead of one
general answer.

Current operational label:

`specialist_owner_routing_needed`

## What Changed

| Area | What changed | Founder takeaway |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Owner routing | Added a domain-to-owner gate. | Ask who owns each unresolved problem: hematology, transfusion medicine, genetics, iron/organ monitoring, transplant/gene-cell therapy, or trial team. |
| Workflow | Added a public-safe notebook for routing order. | Diagnosis, clinical-dependence category, and transfusion-burden quantification remain the highest-priority owner routes. |
| Privacy | Kept owner states label-only. | Public updates should say `owner_requested_records` or similar, not publish names, hospitals, screenshots, or treatment instructions. |

## Practical Rule

If the owner is unknown, do not interpret the domain. Find the owner first.

Allowed public-safe owner states:

- `owner_confirmed`;
- `owner_requested_records`;
- `owner_redirected`;
- `owner_marked_not_applicable`;
- `owner_marked_under_review`;
- `owner_unknown`.

## Boundary

This journal does not provide diagnosis, dosing, transfusion changes,
chelation changes, immune-medicine changes, supplement changes, referral
decisions, or trial eligibility. It only records a safer routing workflow.
