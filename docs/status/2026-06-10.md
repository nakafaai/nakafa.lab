## Current Findings

Pass a quote only if it states recipient fit, model, controls, HbF or `HBG`,
maturation, viability, alpha-globin burden or autophagy, hemolysis or membrane
safety, raw data, cost, timeline, ethics, biosafety, and material constraints.

Hold a quote if the lab can answer only part of the package, especially if
alpha-globin/autophagy, hemolysis, model relevance, controls, raw data, or
cost/timeline are incomplete.

Reject a quote if it is HbF-only, asks for private records or patient samples,
or shifts into treatment selection, trial screening, purchase, import, or
procurement advice.

These sources strengthen assay discipline only. They do not create treatment
advice, clinical access evidence, lab contact permission, or a patient-specific
