# Hypothesis Card: Hydroxyurea Evidence Map

Status: draft evidence map
Evidence label: not a treatment recommendation

## Claim

Hydroxyurea may reduce transfusion burden or improve hemoglobin in selected
beta-thalassemia patients, but response is heterogeneous and must be reviewed by
a hematologist.

## Mechanism

- HbF induction.
- Possible stress erythropoiesis and erythroid effects.
- May be more useful in some NTDT or HbE/beta-thalassemia contexts than as a
  universal TDT solution.

## Evidence To Extract Next

- TDT meta-analysis effect sizes and study quality.
- NTDT meta-analysis effect sizes and study quality.
- Genotype-specific response signals.
- Pediatric versus adult response.
- Response durability and monitoring requirements.

## Safety Questions

- Myelosuppression.
- Infection risk.
- Pregnancy/teratogenicity counseling.
- Liver and kidney monitoring.
- Interaction with current chelation and autoimmune treatment.

## Falsification Criteria

Reject as a priority if high-quality evidence shows no meaningful transfusion
burden reduction in the relevant genotype/phenotype or unacceptable safety risk.
