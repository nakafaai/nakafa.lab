## Current Findings

- The useful discovery is methodological: ClinicalTrials.gov broad regional
  location strings can produce false negatives.
- Future Asia surveillance should run country-specific queries for China,
  Hong Kong, India, Indonesia, Japan, Korea, Malaysia, Singapore, Thailand,
  Turkey, Iran, Pakistan, Russia, and Saudi Arabia instead of relying on
  `Asia`.
- CS-101, BD211, BRL-101, KL003, LentiRed, YOLT-204, luspatercept, and related
  records are source-radar benchmarks only.
- Case-001 branch selection remains blocked until private packet readiness and
  qualified clinician review change.
