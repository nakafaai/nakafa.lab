## Message Draft

Use one concise paragraph:

> I am preparing a de-identified, public-source-backed question. I am not asking
> for treatment, dosing, eligibility, import, or legal advice in this message.
> The narrow question is: [insert one owner-matched question]. Which public-safe
> source or process should be checked before escalation?

## Safe State

Choose one:
- `clinical_first_contact_ready`;
- `pharmacy_first_contact_ready`;
- `regulatory_first_contact_ready`;
- `manufacturer_first_contact_ready`;
- `trial_first_contact_ready`;
- `financial_first_contact_ready`;
- `not_case_eligibility`;
- `release_blocked`.

## Release Check

- One owner only; split multi-owner questions before sending.
- No raw records, screenshots, doctor messages, identifiers, exact dates, or
  private financial documents.
- No patient-specific eligibility claim.
- No treatment, dosing, travel, import, or legal instruction.
