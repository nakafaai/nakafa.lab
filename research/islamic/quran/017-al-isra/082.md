# Quran Anchor: Al-Isra 17:82

Date checked: 2026-04-27
Evidence label: Quran anchor + tafsir intake

## Scope

This note documents a Quran-as-healing verse. It does not identify a medicine
or thalassemia intervention.

## Working Reading

The verse describes the Quran as healing and mercy for believers. For Nakafa
Lab, this belongs primarily to the spiritual-healing and ethical-guidance lane.

## Tafsir Intake

The Ibn Kathir reading available through Alim describes the Quran as removing
heart diseases such as doubt, hypocrisy, shirk, confusion, and inclination
toward falsehood. This is directly relevant to research ethics: false certainty
and wishful claims are heart-level risks in a desperate medical search.

## Research Guardrail

This verse supports patience, faith, and clarity. It should not be used to skip
clinical care or to promote an untested thalassemia remedy.
