## Safe State

Choose one:
- `role_claim_received`;
- `scope_matched`;
- `credential_or_source_needed`;
- `private_intro_ready`;
- `repo_task_ready`;
- `release_blocked`;
- `blocked_raw_record_request`;
- `blocked_treatment_advice`.

## Release Check

- No raw records, screenshots, doctor messages, or identifiers.
- No names, exact dates, hospitals, clinicians, phone numbers, accession
  numbers, or local paths.
- No public treatment advice.
- No case eligibility claim from registry presence alone.
