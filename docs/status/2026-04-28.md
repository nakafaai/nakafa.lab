# Daily Research Journal: 2026-04-28

Audience: founder / engineer
Status: active research summary, not a final conclusion and not medical advice

## TL;DR

Nakafa Lab has not found a new medicine.

Today's important update: the novelty claim is narrower. After `VGB-Ex01`
became the closest editing-registry benchmark, `CS-101` / `NCT06024876` is now a
stronger Asia clinical base-editing benchmark because a 2026 Nature report
described five phase 1 patients who stopped transfusion after autologous CD34+
base editing. `NCT07338344` remains the closest non-editing pharmacologic
benchmark because it tests luspatercept plus low-dose thalidomide with
transfusion-burden, HbF/gamma-globin, hemolysis, iron, and safety endpoints.

Case-001 also gained a practical iron/chelation gate today: weekly transfusion
plus reported daily chelation cannot be interpreted without ferritin trend, LIC,
cardiac `T2*`, chelator identity, toxicity monitoring, and organ-risk records.
The case gates are now compressed into one minimum hematologist packet so the
doctor conversation can start from a single page before opening the longer
brief and question sheet. A public case-data release gate now sits in front of
future case updates so raw records, identifiers, and local source paths do not
enter the public repository. The genotype blocker is now sharpened into a
molecular-record escalation gate: historical HPLC transfusion proximity, `HBB`,
`HBA1/HBA2`, HPFH/delta-beta/HBG context, and family fallback records.
The transfusion blocker is now sharpened into a quantification gate:
case-001 remains `transfusion_burden_unknown` until dates, body weight,
transfused volume, red-cell fraction, Hb increment, and estimated iron input
are documented for clinician review.

That means the Nakafa question is no longer: "Has anyone combined HbF and
clinical endpoints?" The stronger question is:

> Can we find a candidate that is more affordable, safer, and lab-validatable
> for HbF/F-cell rescue plus alpha-globin/autophagy, maturation, hemolysis,
> iron-risk, and access gates?

## Research Direction Status

The strongest lane remains:

`HPFH-like HbF/F-cell rescue + alpha-globin/autophagy cleanup + preserved erythroid maturation + no mature red-cell hemolysis`.

But its novelty must be compared against two proximity gates:

- `CS-101` and `VGB-Ex01`: editing benchmarks.
- `NCT07338344`: non-editing pharmacologic benchmark.

## Next Practical Action

1. Use the proximity novelty gate for every new candidate: is the candidate
   clearly different from the `CS-101`, `VGB-Ex01`, and luspatercept +
   thalidomide benchmarks?
2. Do not promote natural or supplement candidates before they pass identity,
   HbF/F-cell, alpha-globin/autophagy, maturation, hemolysis, and safety gates.
3. Keep the doctor-facing case packet and lab assay quote as the priority,
   because patient-relevance claims are invalid without them.
4. If a lab can add one expansion probe, PF-06409577 / `PRKAB1`-AMPK beta1
   remains the most coherent assay probe, not a therapy.
5. For case-001 specifically, ask the hematologist to resolve the high-HbF
   genotype gate: historical HPLC transfusion proximity, `HBB`, `HBA1/HBA2`,
   and HPFH/delta-beta or HBG-modifier context.
6. Also resolve the immune/transfusion gate: antibody screen, named
   alloantibodies, DAT/direct Coombs specificity, matching policy, red-cell
   phenotype/genotype, delayed reaction history, spleen status, and
   transfusion volume/Hb trend.
7. Quantify transfusion burden from private logs: dates, body weight,
   transfused volume, red-cell fraction, pre/post Hb, annual `ml/kg/year`, pure
   red-cell volume, and estimated daily iron input.
8. Resolve the iron/chelation organ-risk gate: ferritin trend, LIC, cardiac
   `T2*`, chelator identity and plan, toxicity monitoring, endocrine/bone
   screen, transfusion iron-input records, and ferritin-confounder context.
9. Use the minimum hematologist packet as the first page for any doctor
   conversation, then attach the longer clinician brief and question sheet.
10. Run the public case-data release checklist before adding new case facts to
   the repo.
11. Keep running the language gate after prose, notebook, template, or script
    changes so public repo content stays English-only.
