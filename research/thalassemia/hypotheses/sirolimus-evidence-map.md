# Hypothesis Card: Sirolimus Evidence Map

Status: draft evidence map
Evidence label: not a treatment recommendation

## Claim

Low-dose sirolimus may induce gamma-globin expression in selected beta-
thalassemia patients, but clinical benefit and safety require deeper review.

## Mechanism

- mTOR pathway modulation.
- Reported gamma-globin mRNA increase in the Sirthalaclin pilot trial.
- Possible interaction with erythroid precursor biology.

## Evidence To Extract Next

- Sirthalaclin patient inclusion criteria.
- Genotype distribution.
- HbF, hemoglobin, transfusion, and quality-of-life outcomes.
- Blood sirolimus concentrations.
- Adverse events and exclusion criteria.

## Safety Questions

- Immunosuppression and infection risk.
- Drug-level monitoring.
- Proteinuria and liver/kidney constraints.
- Suitability in patients with autoimmune disease.
- Interaction with chelation and other daily medicines.

## Falsification Criteria

Reject as a priority if HbF signal does not translate into meaningful clinical
benefit or if immune/infection risk makes it unsuitable for the target patient
population.
