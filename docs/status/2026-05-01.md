## Current Blockers

- Diagnosis remains `phenotype_only` until the current clinical category and
  molecular context are validated or marked unavailable by a clinician.
- Transfusion burden remains unquantified until the private log has the minimum
  fields listed below and a sufficient observation window.
- Unit-count-only records need a clinician or blood-bank conversion rule before
  they can support volume or pure red-cell calculations.
- Short logs with fewer than two rows or less than 28 days are format-debug
  only, not interpretable burden evidence.
- Immune and blood-bank context remains `immune_transfusion_packet_missing`
  until antibody, DAT/direct Coombs, matching, crossmatch, reaction, spleen, and
  hemolysis context are packeted.
- Iron, chelation, and organ-risk context remains `iron_packet_missing` until
  ferritin trend, LIC MRI, cardiac `T2*`, chelator identity, monitoring, and
  organ-risk data are indexed for clinician review.
- Referral readiness remains `advanced_therapy_referral_packet_missing` until a
  qualified specialist can screen a complete enough packet.
