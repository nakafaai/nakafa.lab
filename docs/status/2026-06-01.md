## Current Findings

- Hydroxyurea and melittin stay `benchmark_only`. Hydroxyurea is useful as a
  positive HbF comparator, and melittin is useful only as a hemolysis hazard
  control if a lab accepts that role.
- Purified resveratrol, sirolimus, and PF-06409577 are
  `assay_ready_for_quote` only as preclinical assay items with identity, batch,
  vehicle, model, endpoint, safety, and raw-data requirements.
- Standardized 6-shogaol-rich ginger extract remains held until extract
  standardization, marker assay, batch controls, and support-only endpoint
  wording are resolved.
- `T-BDMC`-like curcuminoid chemistry remains held until exact structure,
  procurement identity, and batch control are resolved.
- PubChem identity checks resolve named compound identity for hydroxyurea,
  resveratrol, sirolimus, 6-shogaol, melittin, and PF-06409577. This does not
  prove efficacy, clinical benefit, Indonesia access, or case-001 relevance.

## Current Blockers

  guidance, trial screening, import guidance, travel guidance, or
