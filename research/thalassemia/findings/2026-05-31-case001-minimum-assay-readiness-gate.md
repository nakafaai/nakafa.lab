# Case-001 Minimum Assay Readiness Gate

Date checked: 2026-05-31
Evidence label: assay-method, literature, workflow, and privacy synthesis, not
medical advice

## Direct Answer

After the May 30 promotion gate, the next blocker is assay readiness. A
candidate with a named gap and endpoint is still not ready for lab outreach
unless the model, controls, stop rules, raw-data plan, and privacy boundary are
explicit.

Current operational label:

`case001_minimum_assay_readiness_gate_ready`

## Minimum Gate

Before lab quote or partner discussion, require:

1. exact candidate identity or an identity-gap label;
2. candidate category and target affordability gap;
3. model context and limitations;
4. positive, vehicle, negative, and hazard controls as appropriate;
5. endpoint set for HbF/HBG, maturation, viability, hemolysis, chain balance,
   free alpha-globin, and claimed autophagy;
6. stop rules and falsification test;
7. raw-data, replicate, ethics, and privacy boundaries.

## Current Evidence Read

The source refresh supports this gate. Recent assay papers provide better
screening tools and disease models, but they do not create a patient-specific
therapy claim. HUDEP2 and reporter systems are screening contexts. Hemolysis
assays are safety filters. Disease-specific models improve translational
relevance only when the genotype, model limits, and endpoints match the claim.

## Decision Rule

No candidate moves from "promote to gap review" to "lab quote" unless the
minimum assay package is complete.

Case-001 relevance remains blocked until qualified specialist review.

## Islamic Motivation Boundary

Quran 16:43 supports expert review. Quran 13:17 supports discarding weak
signals. Quran 55:7-9 supports measured claims. These are process anchors, not
biomedical evidence for any therapy.
