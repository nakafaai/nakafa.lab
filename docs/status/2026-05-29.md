## Current Findings

Existing curative biology is real, but realistic access still has gaps:

- HSCT depends on donor route, expert centers, conditioning, follow-up, and
  risk review.
- Gene-cell therapy depends on collection, manufacturing or editing,
  myeloablation, product access, expert centers, follow-up, cost, and durability
  evidence.
- Non-curative drugs can benchmark transfusion-burden reduction but must not be
  framed as cure.
- Standard-care stabilization remains a prerequisite before advanced-route
  interpretation.
- Registry records remain benchmark context unless a qualified specialist asks a
  specific screening question.
