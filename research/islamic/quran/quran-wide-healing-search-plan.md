# Quran-Wide Healing Search Plan

Date checked: 2026-04-26
Evidence label: Islamic research method

## Purpose

This plan keeps the Islamic lane from stopping at one verse. Quran 16:68-69 is
the bee anchor, but the whole Quran should be searched for healing, illness,
mercy, guidance, harm, lawful provision, and tests.

## First Root And Theme Set

- `shifa` / healing: 9:14, 10:57, 16:69, 17:82, 26:80, 41:44.
- illness and distress: `marad`, Ayyub, hardship, patience, mercy.
- provision and lawful/tayyib intake: food, purity, harm avoidance.
- signs in creation: animals, plants, water, mountains, pairs, balance.
- ethics of action: do not harm, do not claim knowledge without evidence,
  consult people of knowledge.
- measurement and balance: `mizan`, justice, fair measure, and avoiding
  transgression in claims.
- benefit and attrition: separating what benefits people from foam/noise and
  false starts.

## Method

For each verse:

1. Record Arabic text, Indonesian translation, and English translation.
2. Record tafsir separately from translation.
3. Mark whether the verse is theological, ethical, natural-world observation, or
   a possible biomedical hypothesis anchor.
4. Do not convert a verse into a treatment claim without scientific evidence.
5. Ask what testable question, if any, follows from the verse.

## Known Healing Anchors

- Quran 9:14: healing of hearts in a conflict context.
- Quran 10:57: Quran as healing for what is in the breasts.
- Quran 16:69: bee-derived drink with healing for people.
- Quran 17:82: Quran as healing and mercy for believers.
- Quran 26:80: Ibrahim says that when he is ill, Allah heals him.
- Quran 41:44: Quran as guidance and healing for believers.

## Natural Material Expansion

The first natural-material map is now tracked in
[Quranic natural material candidate map](quranic-natural-material-candidate-map.md).
It expands the hypothesis lens from bees to plants, fruits, water, provision,
and signs in creation while preserving the rule that Quranic mention is not
pharmacology proof.

The measurement-and-balance anchor is tracked in
[Ar-Rahman 55:7-9](055-ar-rahman/007-009.md). It supports quantitative scoring
and safety gates rather than a new natural-material candidate.

The benefit-versus-foam anchor is tracked in
[Ar-Ra'd 13:17](013-ar-rad/017.md). It supports disciplined candidate
attrition: promote what remains beneficial after measurement, and discard noisy
signals.

## Research Guardrail

The Quran can guide our worldview, ethics, patience, hope, and hypothesis
generation. It does not remove the need for mechanism, toxicity, dose,
reproducibility, and clinician review.
