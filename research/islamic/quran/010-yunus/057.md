# Quran Anchor: Yunus 10:57

Date checked: 2026-04-27
Evidence label: Quran anchor + tafsir intake

## Scope

This note documents a `shifa` verse whose immediate frame is moral and spiritual
healing. It is not a biomedical treatment claim.

## Working Reading

The verse describes revelation as admonition, healing for what is in the
breasts, guidance, and mercy for believers. The research implication is ethical:
the lab must seek truth, avoid false claims, and keep the heart disciplined
while pursuing external means.

## Tafsir Intake

Quran.com's Ibn Kathir page links this passage to boundaries around what Allah
has made lawful and unlawful. The Arabic Ibn Kathir entry frames the cure as
removal of doubts and impurities from the heart. This supports spiritual and
ethical healing, not a molecule claim.

## Research Guardrail

Do not convert this verse into a claim that Quran recitation, honey, or a bee
product biologically cures thalassemia. The verse can shape intention and
ethics; biomedical hypotheses still require separate evidence.
