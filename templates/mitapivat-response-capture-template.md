## Release Check

- No raw reply text.
- No private emails, phone numbers, names, hospital contacts, or screenshots.
- No raw lab files, doctor messages, or exact private dates.
- No patient-specific treatment, dosing, import, travel, or eligibility advice.
- No adverse-event narrative or product-quality detail tied to a person.
- No legal conclusion or instruction.
