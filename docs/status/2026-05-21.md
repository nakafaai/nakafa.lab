## Current Findings

The May 21 source check did not loosen the evidence boundary. TIF 2025 remains
the clinical-context baseline for transfusion and iron/organ discussion. FDA
AQVESME remains adult-label and safety context. Pediatric mitapivat remains
watchlist-only.

ClinicalTrials.gov API checks showed `NCT07506863` pediatric TDT and
`NCT07517133` pediatric NTDT are not-yet-recruiting records with no posted
results, last updated 2026-04-08, under version 2026-05-20. `NCT04770779`
adult TDT remains active-not-recruiting with posted results, last updated
2026-04-24, under version 2026-05-20.

The practical blocker is conversation order. The first question to a doctor is
not "can we use mitapivat?" The ordered question is: are the core labels correct,
which records are missing, are the seven domains ready, and only then is the
mitapivat lane worth reviewing at all?
