# Discovery Pipeline

Date: 2026-04-26

## Purpose

This is the Nakafa Lab path from idea to credible research candidate.

## Stages

1. Source-backed hypothesis.
2. Target or endpoint mapping.
3. Assay selection.
4. Safety screen.
5. Affordability and access screen.
6. Clinician and domain-expert review.
7. Preclinical validation plan.
8. Publication-ready evidence note.

## Cure-Oriented Endpoint Priority

- Durable transfusion independence.
- Reduced transfusion burden.
- Increased HbF or useful hemoglobin.
- Better globin-chain balance.
- Improved erythroid maturation.
- Better red-cell survival without hemolysis.
- Reduced iron toxicity risk.

## Candidate Intake Rule

Every candidate must name its category:

- approved drug;
- repurposed drug;
- investigational drug;
- purified molecule;
- standardized extract;
- natural product mixture;
- biomaterial;
- gene/cell therapy concept.

Natural product mixtures and bee-derived materials require stronger identity and
safety controls before they can be compared with known drugs.
