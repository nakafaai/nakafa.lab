  `ready_*` or `missing_*` labels.

## Patient-Context Data Still Needed

- Clinician-confirmed diagnosis, genotype, phenotype label, and molecular
  report status.
- Transfusion dates, interval, units or volume, red-cell fraction if available,
  pre-transfusion hemoglobin, response pattern, and annualized burden.
- Blood-bank and immune history: matching policy, antibody screen,
  alloantibodies, DAT/direct Coombs context, reaction history, spleen context,
  and crossmatch issues.
- Iron and organ-risk state: ferritin trend, liver iron, cardiac `T2*`,
  chelator identity, toxicity monitoring, endocrine/cardiac/liver notes, and
  clinician interpretation.
- HLA typing, donor availability, transplant or gene-cell center access, payer
  route, geography, travel constraints, fertility counseling status, and
  patient/family consent boundaries.
- Ethics, biosafety, import, material-transfer, private-record, and sample
  constraints before any workflow touches patient samples or raw private data.
