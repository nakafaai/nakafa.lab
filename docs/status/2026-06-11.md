## Current Findings

The strongest current finding is a process finding: Nakafa Lab has a usable
quote-acceptance rule, but not an approved quote recipient and not an accepted
quote.

Pass a quote only if recipient class, model, controls, HbF or `HBG`, maturation,
viability, alpha-globin burden or autophagy, hemolysis or membrane safety, raw
data, cost, timeline, ethics, biosafety, and material constraints are stated.

Hold a quote if the lab can answer part of the package but key endpoints, model
relevance, controls, raw data, or cost and timeline are incomplete.

Reject a quote if it is HbF-only, asks for private records or patient samples,
or shifts into treatment selection, trial screening, purchase, import, or
procurement advice.

The first quote table remains public-safe preclinical work. Hydroxyurea is a
positive HbF comparator; purified resveratrol is a seed; sirolimus is an
mTOR/autophagy comparator; melittin is a hemolysis hazard control; PF-06409577
is an AMPK beta1 expansion probe. These roles are not treatment
recommendations.

`T-BDMC` and standardized 6-shogaol-rich ginger extract remain held out of the
first quote until identity, batch, standardization, and safety-window issues
are resolved.
