# Case-001 Mitapivat Indonesia Access Evidence Gap

Date checked: 2026-05-24
Evidence label: Indonesia access evidence-gap gate, not medical advice

## Direct Answer

The next blocker is local access verification.

Mitapivat now has adult thalassemia approval context in multiple foreign
jurisdictions, but the May 24 public pass did not find a public Indonesia
medicine-registration record or an Indonesia ClinicalTrials.gov location for
mitapivat-thalassemia.

Current operational label:

`mitapivat_indonesia_access_evidence_gap_ready`

## Source Refresh

- Cek BPOM public medicine searches for `mitapivat`, `Pyrukynd`, and `Aqvesme`
  returned zero filtered records in this pass.
- The BPOM drug-registration portal is still the official public entry point,
  so this is an evidence gap, not proof of non-registration.
- ClinicalTrials.gov returned six `mitapivat thalassemia` records. The May 24
  parse found no Indonesia locations. Adult TDT and adult NTDT records did show
  regional locations including Malaysia, Thailand, Taiwan, Saudi Arabia, and the
  United Arab Emirates.
- FDA, EU, Saudi, and UAE adult approval contexts sharpen the owner question,
  but they do not establish Indonesian availability, affordability, safety fit,
  or individual fit.

## Owner Question

Use this public-safe question only after clinician label review:

Which owner can verify whether mitapivat is registered, formulary-accessible,
special-access eligible, manufacturer-supported, reimbursed, or clearly
unavailable in the relevant Indonesia care pathway?

- `treating_hematologist`;
- `hospital_pharmacy`;
- `regulatory_affairs_or_bpom_facing_owner`;
- `manufacturer_medical_information`;
- `payer_or_financial_assistance_owner`.

## Stop Conditions

Stop before public release if a response contains:

- raw records, screenshots, or exact private dates;
- doctor messages, owner replies, clinician names, hospital names, or contact
  details;
- patient-specific treatment instructions;
- dose, access, import, travel, or trial-screening instructions.

## Biomedical Boundary

This gate does not recommend mitapivat, decide local access, decide import or
travel, screen a trial, or replace a hematologist. It only records that local
public evidence is missing and names the owner categories needed to verify it.
