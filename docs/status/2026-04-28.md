# Daily Research Journal: 2026-04-28

Audience: founder / engineer
Status: active research summary, not a final conclusion and not medical advice

## TL;DR

Nakafa Lab has not found a new medicine, and nothing here changes patient care.

Today's most important update is that the novelty bar is higher. `CS-101` is
now the strongest Asia-based base-editing benchmark because the repo has a
phase 1 clinical report tied to `NCT06024876` and a new phase 2 registry
escalation in `NCT07489196`. That blocks weak "HbF rescue is new" claims.

The stronger Nakafa question is now narrower:

> Can we find an affordable, safer, non-editing or lower-infrastructure
> candidate that can pass the same kind of multi-endpoint validation gate:
> HbF/F-cells, alpha-globin/autophagy, erythroid maturation, viability,
> hemolysis, iron-risk, and access?

Case-001 work also moved from broad curiosity into record-gated execution. The
case remains blocked until genotype, transfusion burden, immune/blood-bank
status, iron/chelation organ risk, and referral-readiness records are complete
enough for hematologist review.

## Strongest Research Lanes

1. **Minimum hematologist packet.** This is the closest practical output:
   organize subtype/genotype, transfusion burden, immune/blood-bank, iron,
   organ, spleen, infection, fertility, prior therapy, and access records.
2. **Proximity novelty gate.** Every candidate must explain what it adds beyond
   `CS-101`, `NCT07489196`, `VGB-Ex01`, YOLT-204, luspatercept plus
   thalidomide, and AND017 for oral Hb/transfusion/iron-marker claims.
3. **Dual-readout assay.** The lab panel must measure HbF/F-cells beside
   alpha-globin burden or autophagy, maturation, viability, apoptosis, and
   mature red-cell hemolysis.
4. **Stable first quote panel.** Keep hydroxyurea, purified resveratrol,
   sirolimus, standardized 6-shogaol-rich ginger extract, and melittin hazard
   control as the first quote structure.
5. **Conditional expansion probe.** Add PF-06409577 / `PRKAB1`-AMPK beta1 only
   if the lab can document material identity and support the full safety gate.
6. **Privacy-safe case workflow.** Use ignored private intake files, extraction
   worksheets, timeline precision labels, and public-release checks before any
   new case facts enter the public repo.

## Patient-Context Data Still Needed

Collect only de-identified, public-safe summaries for the repo. Keep raw
records in ignored private storage.

- exact diagnosis label: `TDT`, `NTDT`, HbE/beta, alpha, mixed, or unresolved;
- `HBB`, `HBA1/HBA2`, HPFH/delta-beta, and HBG-modifier context when available;
- HPLC/electrophoresis fractions with transfusion proximity noted;
- body weight, transfusion dates, volume or unit count, red-cell fraction,
  product type, pre-transfusion Hb, and post-transfusion Hb or increment;
- antibody screen, named alloantibodies, DAT/direct Coombs specificity,
  crossmatch issues, red-cell antigen phenotype/genotype, and reaction history;
- ferritin trend, liver iron concentration MRI, cardiac `T2*`, chelator name,
  dose, schedule, adherence, side effects, and kidney/liver/blood-count
  monitoring;
- spleen status, hemolysis markers, autoimmune diagnosis, immune medicines,
  infection/liver-virus context, endocrine/cardiac/liver/kidney/bone screen;
- whether a clinician has already discussed HSCT, gene therapy, CRISPR therapy,
  luspatercept, mitapivat, hydroxyurea, or trial referral.
