- Which erythroid or red-cell model is available?
- Which HbF, `HBG`, maturation, viability, and hemolysis readouts are possible?
- Which positive, negative, vehicle, batch, and toxicity controls are included?
- What raw data and method metadata can be exported?
- What sample, ethics, biosafety, MTA, import, and material constraints apply?
- What cost and timeline range is realistic for a small first feasibility run?
