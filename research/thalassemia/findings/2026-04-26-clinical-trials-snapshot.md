# Finding: ClinicalTrials.gov Snapshot

Date checked: 2026-04-26
Evidence label: Registry snapshot

## Query

ClinicalTrials.gov API v2 was queried for `Thalassemia`.

## Working Conclusion

The trial landscape is active globally, but the first registry pass did not find
currently recruiting or not-yet-recruiting thalassemia trials located in
Indonesia.

## Snapshot Results

- `Thalassemia` with status `RECRUITING` or `NOT_YET_RECRUITING`: 96 studies.
- Same query with location `Indonesia`: 0 studies.
- All-status query with location `Indonesia`: 6 studies, mostly completed or
  unknown status.

## Notable Global Trial Themes

- Gene-editing or gene-modified autologous stem-cell therapy.
- Allogeneic transplant conditioning and immune suppression.
- Mitapivat pediatric transfusion-dependent thalassemia study.
- Supportive and complication-focused studies.

## Indonesia-Relevant Completed/Unknown Registry Examples

- Thalassemia knowledge in young people of Indonesia.
- Alpha-tocopherol study in beta-thalassemia major.
- Pneumococcal vaccination in splenectomised thalassemia major patients.
- Zinc supplementation and cellular immunity.
- Deferiprone early-treatment study with an Indonesia site.
- Immunomodulatory vitamin D in thalassemia.

## Research Implication

ClinicalTrials.gov alone is not enough for Indonesia access mapping. Next, check
local hospitals, BPOM status, BPJS coverage details, specialist centers, and
regional trials not indexed under Indonesia.
